ABOUT RICK'S CAMPS
The All-Skills Camps are designed for novice to elite players ages 13-19. Players must be at least 13 years of age or entering the 8th grade. During the four-day camp, players will benefit from intensive drills, competition, and instruction. Participants will receive expert coaching and extensive training in competitive volleyball fundamentals, tactics, and advanced conditioning techniques. All of our courts are indoor. The camp is open to any and all entrants limited only by number, age, and gender.
  • 10-to-1 Player-to-Coach Ratio
  • Emphasis on Proper Fundamentals
  • Excellent Individual Skills Development Sessions
  • Nightly Competition
  • Indoor Courts
  • Camp T-Shirt

HOUSING AND MEALS
Campers will stay at either Tropicana Gardens or Tropicana del Norte Student Housing in Isla Vista. More information (plus driving directions) to come. The dorm has a swimming pool, recreational room and laundry facilities. Campers are supervised by camp counselors. Healthy and hearty meals are planned by a professional nutritionist. Meals will begin with dinner on the first day and end with breakfast on the last day. Linens are NOT included. Campers have the option of bringing their own linens (beds are Twin/Single XL; sleeping bags are fine) or renting a linen packet at check-in for $25 which includes 1 blanket, 1 pillow, 2 sheets, 2 towels, and a hand towel. (Individual linen items can be purchased for $5 each at the desk.) For more information on Tropicana Student Living, click here: TROPICANA STUDENT LIVING INFO

TRANSPORTATION
Campers must arrange their own transportation to the camp on Day 1 and from the camp on Day 4.

CANCELLATION POLICY
If you cancel your registration by one month before Day 1, you may receive a refund less a $75 administration fee. Sorry, there are no refunds/credits if cancellation occurs after one month before Day 1, even if player is sick and/or injured and has a doctor's note. Please do not register if you do not accept this policy.

MEDICAL CARE
A certified athletic trainer is in attendance during all practice sessions. The cost of any treatment will be billed to the injured camper's insurance carrier. Medical Release and Liability form must be received prior to participation in camp.

CHECK-IN / CHECK-OUT INFORMATION
OVERNIGHT CAMPERS
Check-in is from 1-2 p.m. on Day 1, at either Tropicana Gardens or Tropicana del Norte Student Housing in Isla Vista. More information (plus driving directions) to come once we receive our dorm assignment. Check-out is at noon on Day 4, also at the dorm.

DAY CAMPERS
Day campers are to be dropped off and picked up each day at the UCSB Events Center. (See driving directions below.) The day camp is from 9 a.m. to 8:30 p.m. except on the first day (camp begins at 2 p.m. on Day 1) and the last day (camp ends at noon on Day 4). Lunches and dinners will be provided. IMPORTANT: Day campers must be signed out at the end of each day and picked up on time, and camp ends at noon on Day 4 for everyone.


DRIVING DIRECTIONS

OVERNIGHT CAMPERS
Driving Directions to Tropicana Gardens or Tropicana del Norte student housing will be posted closer to camp dates.

DAY CAMPERS
Driving Directions to UCSB Events Center:

From the South:
Travel north on U.S. Highway 101. Take the Airport/UCSB exit (Route 217) off U.S. 101 approximately 8 miles north of Santa Barbara to the campus entrance. When you arrive at the East Gate turn right on Mesa Rd. Go to the 3rd traffic light and turn left on Ocean Rd. Go to the 1st traffic light and turn right, you are still on Ocean Road. Go to the 2nd traffic light. The Events Center is on your left.


From the North:
Travel south on U.S. Highway 101. Take the Storke Road/Glen Annie Road exit off U.S. 101 approximately 12 miles north of Santa Barbara. Turn right on Storke Road and proceed two miles to El Colegio Road. Turn left on El Colegio Road and proceed to the campus entrance. When you arrive at the West Gate, continue straight on El Collegio to the first traffic light. The Events Center is directly in front of you.
