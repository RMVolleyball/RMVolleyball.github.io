In addition to Rick McLaughlin, the camp features an outstanding staff of successful high school, club, and collegiate volleyball coaches, as well as elite volleyball players, including members of the UCSB Men's Volleyball Team coaching staff and players. With an approximate player/coach ratio of ten-to-one, each participant is ensured high quality, individualized instruction.
